Welcome to Mangerie Online, your kaleidoscopic view into your fondest memories!

Where has the time gone? It seems like only yesterday you were hanging around with the boys! Why not have a look into your kaleidoscope and revisit all of those wonderful moments?

context

Mangerie Online is the online successor to Mangerie. To make the kaleidoscope more accessible, I made this online variant, with a fancy new interface and way more pictures! You can play it locally (mobile supported) or on itch (desktop only).

Warning: you will get the best results on resolutions that clearly embrace either landscape or portrait orientation. Devices that approach a 1/1 aspect ratio (iPad Air and similar), you will some parts of the interface.
