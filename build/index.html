Welcome to Mangerie Online, your online kaleidoscope!
To start playing, pull on the large lever of the kaleidoscope. This will influence its internal mirrors, adjusting their angles to create more facets. You can also use the mouse wheel to interact with the lever.

After angling the mirrors, you can start rotating the base image to achieve true kaleidoscopic splendor! Do this by tugging on the arrowed lever, or simply on the edge of the circle will work as well.
